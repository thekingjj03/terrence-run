HOW TO PLAY

🎮 CONTROLS

WASD or Arrow Keys move Terrance. Press Space for a burst. On touch screens, drag to move and double-tap to burst.

🃏 CARD BINDER

Collect card pickups or open packs. In the binder, click an owned card and equip its special ability before a run.

🪙 COINS & SHOP

Score points to earn Terrance Coins. Spend them on card packs, skins, and accessories. Duplicate cards convert into coins.

🛡️ PICKUPS

Coins score points, Mini Terrance gives a shield, and merch props give bonuses. Avoid each level's hazards.

🧴 GIANT GLUE BOTTLE

Reach the boss score to trigger the fight. Dodge glue shots and collect Clean-Up Sponges to damage the bottle until it is defeated.

🏆 COMPLETION REWARD

Discover all ten Prototype Series cards to unlock Prototype Master Terrance and a 500 coin reward.
